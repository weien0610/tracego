Pricing Details

Currency & payment

Pricing is shown in New Taiwan Dollars (NT$). Premium is also available in USD (~$4.99/month) and other supported currencies. Local taxes (VAT, sales tax) are calculated at checkout based on your billing country.

Billing cycle

Premium is billed monthly. Your subscription auto-renews on the same day each month. You will receive an email receipt for each charge.

Free trial

The 7-day free trial begins when you start your Premium subscription. No charge is made during the trial period. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your card will be charged for the first month.

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account portal. After cancellation, Premium features remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. No further charges will be made.

Refunds

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on your first paid month. See our Refund Policy for full details.

Payment processor

Payments are securely processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. Paddle handles billing, taxes, and refunds on our behalf. We do not store any credit card information on our servers.
