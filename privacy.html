1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how TraceGo ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our desktop software ("the Software") and our website at weien0610.github.io/tracego ("the Site").

This policy applies to:

  • Visitors to our website
  • Users of the free TraceGo desktop application
  • Subscribers to TraceGo Premium

2. Information We Collect

📧 Account & subscription data (only if you subscribe to Premium)

Email address

To deliver your license key and subscription notices.

Name and billing country

Required for tax calculation by our payment processor.

Payment information

Card details are collected and stored exclusively by Paddle. We never see or store your full card number.

License key activation status

The Software periodically validates your license against our server (every 7 days). This check transmits your license key and a hashed device identifier — no personal information.

📊 Limited diagnostic data (anonymous)

Software version & OS version

Sent during license validation to determine update eligibility. Cannot be linked to your identity.

Crash reports

If the Software crashes, an anonymized crash log may be uploaded to help us diagnose bugs. You can opt out in Settings.

🌐 Website data

Server logs

GitHub Pages (our website host) may keep standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and referrer URL, retained per GitHub's privacy policy.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We deliberately designed TraceGo to be privacy-respecting. The following data is processed entirely on your local Windows computer and never transmitted to our servers:

  • ❌ The GPS coordinates you set or simulate
  • ❌ Your real device location
  • ❌ The location of your iPhone or any paired devices
  • ❌ Bookmarks, saved routes, or GPX files you create
  • ❌ Apple ID, iCloud account, or any iOS account credentials
  • ❌ Photos, contacts, messages, or any other iOS device data
  • ❌ Browsing history or behavior on third-party apps

The pairing trust file required to communicate with your iPhone is stored locally on your Windows computer and is never uploaded.

4. How We Use Information

We use the limited data we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Software (license validation, updates)
  • Process payments and issue receipts (via Paddle)
  • Communicate with you about your subscription, security issues, or material changes to our service
  • Diagnose and fix bugs (via opt-out crash reports)
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax records, fraud prevention)

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising or behavioral profiling
  • Share your data with affiliate partners

5. Sharing & Third Parties

We share limited data only with the following service providers, each acting as a data processor on our behalf:

Paddle.com Market Limited (Merchant of Record)

Processes all subscription payments. Receives your name, email, billing address, and payment information. Paddle's privacy policy: paddle.com/legal/privacy

Email service provider

Delivers transactional emails (license keys, receipts, subscription notices). Email address is the only data shared.

GitHub Pages

Hosts our public website. Standard server logs only.

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

6. Website Cookies & Analytics

Our website (weien0610.github.io/tracego) does not currently use tracking cookies, third-party analytics, or behavioral advertising tools. We may add privacy-respecting analytics in the future and will update this policy if so.

Affiliate links on the website may set cookies on the destination site (e.g., Klook, NordVPN) when you click them. These cookies are governed by the destination site's own privacy policy.

7. Data Retention

  • Active subscribers: We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active.
  • Cancelled subscribers: Account data is retained for up to 24 months after cancellation, then deleted, except where required for tax or legal compliance.
  • Tax and billing records: Retained by Paddle for 7 years per applicable tax law.
  • Crash reports: Retained for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdrawal of consent — Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at king1234t@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

For payment-related data held by Paddle, you can also contact Paddle directly through their privacy portal.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we hold, including:

  • Encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS) for all server communication
  • Hashed and salted license-device bindings (no plain-text storage)
  • Strict access controls on backend systems
  • Reliance on Paddle's PCI-DSS-compliant infrastructure for payment data

No system is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware.

10. Children's Privacy

TraceGo is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. International Transfers

We are based in Taiwan. By using TraceGo, you understand that your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, including Taiwan, the United Kingdom (where Paddle is based), and the United States. We rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards where required by law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email (for active subscribers) or by posting a prominent notice on our website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

📧 king1234t@gmail.com

📌 一句話版本

TraceGo 在你電腦本地運作，絕對不會把你設定的 GPS 座標、行動裝置資料傳到我們的伺服器。我們唯一收集的個人資料是付費訂閱時的 email 與帳單資訊（由 Paddle 處理）。

我們會收集

  • 訂閱者的 email、姓名、帳單國家（用於發送授權碼與計算稅金）
  • 授權驗證資料（每 7 天回報一次授權狀態，含雜湊後的裝置識別碼）
  • 可選的當機回報（可在設定關閉）
  • 網站訪客的標準伺服器日誌（由 GitHub Pages 管理）

我們不會收集

  • ❌ 你模擬的 GPS 座標
  • ❌ 你的真實位置
  • ❌ iPhone 中任何資料（Apple ID、相片、聯絡人等）
  • ❌ 你建立的書籤、路徑、GPX 檔

你的權利

你可以隨時要求查閱、更正、刪除我們持有的個人資料，請來信 king1234t@gmail.com，我們將在 30 天內回覆。

資料保存期限

  • 訂閱期間：持續保留
  • 取消訂閱後：最多 24 個月後刪除（稅務記錄除外）
  • 稅務記錄（由 Paddle 保管）：依法保存 7 年
