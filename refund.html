Refunds are issued by Paddle, our payment processor. Funds typically return to your card within 3–10 business days.

1. Free Trial Period

Every new TraceGo Premium subscriber receives a 7-day free trial. During this period:

  • You have full access to all Premium features
  • No charge is made to your payment method
  • You can cancel anytime via your account portal — no questions asked
  • If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be billed

If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your payment method will automatically be charged the standard monthly rate. We recommend evaluating the Software thoroughly during the trial period.

2. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you complete the 7-day free trial and are charged for your first paid month but find that TraceGo does not meet your expectations, you may request a full refund within 14 days of the first paid charge.

✅ What's covered: Your first paid month, in full, no questions asked. We genuinely want you to be satisfied.

Eligibility

  • The refund request must be submitted within 14 calendar days of the first paid charge (not the trial start date)
  • The 14-day money-back guarantee applies to your first paid month only
  • Subsequent monthly charges are not covered by this guarantee, but you may cancel anytime to prevent future charges

3. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us

    Send an email to king1234t@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request".

  2. Include your details

    The email address you used for the subscription, the order ID (if available), and an optional brief reason (helps us improve).

  3. We acknowledge within 2 business days

    We will confirm receipt of your request and notify Paddle to initiate the refund.

  4. Paddle processes the refund

    Paddle issues the refund to your original payment method within 3–5 business days. Total time to see funds in your account: typically 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

  5. Subscription is cancelled

    Your subscription is terminated upon refund. Premium features become unavailable; the Software reverts to Free tier.

You may also contact Paddle directly through the link in your purchase receipt email. Paddle will coordinate with us as needed.

4. Cancellation vs. Refund

These are two different actions:

  • Cancellation stops future billing. Premium features remain active until the end of your current billing cycle. No refund is issued for the unused portion of the current cycle.
  • Refund reverses a charge that was already made. Premium access is terminated immediately upon refund.

You can cancel at any time from your account portal or by emailing us. Refund requests are governed by Section 2 above.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

The 14-day money-back guarantee does not apply in the following situations:

  • Refund is requested more than 14 days after the first paid charge
  • Refund is requested for charges beyond the first paid month (subsequent monthly fees)
  • The account has been terminated for violation of our Terms of Service (e.g., abusive use, fraud)
  • Chargebacks initiated without first contacting us — we always prefer a friendly resolution before bank disputes

⚠️ A note on chargebacks: If you have any concern, please email us before filing a chargeback. We will work in good faith to resolve any issue. Chargebacks filed without prior contact may result in account termination and may be reported to Paddle's fraud system.

6. Statutory Rights

This Refund Policy is offered in addition to, and does not replace, any consumer rights you may have under the mandatory law of your country of residence (for example, the EU Consumer Rights Directive provides a 14-day withdrawal right for digital purchases, with certain conditions).

If your statutory rights provide for a longer or more generous refund period than this policy, those rights apply.

7. Currency & Fees

Refunds are issued in the original currency of payment. Currency conversion fees, foreign transaction fees, or bank fees applied by your card issuer are not refunded by us, as we do not control them.

Tax (VAT, sales tax) collected at the time of purchase is included in the refund.

8. Contact

For all refund requests or questions about this policy, contact:

📧 king1234t@gmail.com

For payment-specific issues, you may also contact Paddle support directly via the link in your receipt email.
