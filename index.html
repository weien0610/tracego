Frequently Asked Questions

Is TraceGo legal to use?

Yes. TraceGo uses Apple's official developer protocols (the same APIs Apple provides to app developers and testers). It does not jailbreak, modify, or hack your device in any way.

Does TraceGo require jailbreaking my iPhone?

No. TraceGo works with stock, unmodified iPhones running iOS 17 and later. No security modifications are required.

What iOS versions are supported?

TraceGo natively supports iOS 17, 18, and the latest 26 releases. We update the underlying tunnel library as Apple releases new iOS versions.

Will my location data be uploaded to your servers?

No. TraceGo runs entirely on your local Windows machine. We do not collect, transmit, or store your location data on any server.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime through your account page. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing cycle, with no further charges.
